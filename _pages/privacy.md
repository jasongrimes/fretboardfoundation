  This is the privacy notice for **book.fretboardfoundation.com**, the online
  edition of the Fretboard Foundation book. It's a companion to the main
  Fretboard Foundation site and is covered by the same
  [Fretboard Foundation Privacy Policy](https://fretboardfoundation.com/privacy).
  This page explains what that means for this site specifically.

  **This site does not track you.**

  - **No analytics or tracking tools.** This site uses no analytics, advertising,
    or cross-site tracking of any kind.
  - **No cookies from us.** It's a static site; it sets no cookies of its own and
    asks you for no personal information.
  - **No account, no sign-up.** You can read the entire book without identifying
    yourself.

  **What still happens at the infrastructure level.** Like any website, this site
  is served by hosting providers that automatically process basic technical
  request data (such as your IP address) in order to deliver pages and keep the
  site secure. We don't use this data to build profiles or for advertising.

  - **GitHub Pages** hosts the static files — see
    [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
  - **Cloudflare** provides content delivery and security, and may set a strictly
    necessary security cookie — see
    [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

  **Links to the main site.** If you follow a link to fretboardfoundation.com — for
  example, to subscribe to the newsletter — that activity is governed by the
  [main Privacy Policy](https://fretboardfoundation.com/privacy), which also covers
  your choices and rights and how to contact us.
